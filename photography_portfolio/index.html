About

"Photography has always been one of my favorite hobbies. But with time it became a passion. My years of work have allowed me experience with all types of lenses and cameras and I understand how a picture can convey more meaning than words are able to. I especially enjoy the challenge of bringing a smile to a cranky little one. I love helping clients to choose backgrounds and poses for their portraits. But what I really enjoy is that I can help to make unforgettable memories for my clients and share with them the best moments in their lives."

Igor is doing photography since 1983, his photos were published in NH Bride Magazine and numerous publications nationally and internationally.
Igor is shooting at the Weddings, Bar / Bat Mitzvahs, and at the Events.
Igor has many happy Clients and good reviews from Photographers, and reasonable prices.

Welcome to North Reading Pediatrics!


Our goal is to provide up-to-date pediatric medicine in a warm and personal atmosphere.

Our practice is a central resource for your child’s medical needs, and we aim to provide accessible, continuous, coordinated, and compassionate care.

Because Dr. Gleyzer is the only practitioner in our office, he oversees every aspect of your child’s primary medical care and will see your child at all visits (with the rare exception when he must ask a covering doctor to see our patients while he attends professional meetings or he is on vacation).

Parents are an important and active part of a successful health care team

It is important that you schedule regular checkups for your child and notify Dr. Gleyzer of all visits with specialists.


Staff


Dr. Vladimir Gleyzer is an American Board of Pediatrics certified pediatrician with over 20 years of experience.

He is assisted by our licensed and highly-skilled Nurse Practitioner Tara Forest
